Project 3 Brief Description

Self

Self is a narrative-driven first-person horror experience centered around a 17-year-old girl who struggles with anxiety and schizophrenia. You play as her, traversing through another dimension where nothing makes sense. You can see the monster by closing your eyes, but the room shifts if you do so for too long.

What I Did:

In this game, I was responsible for the NPC pathfinding using the A* algorithm and for the NPC AI (Behavior Tree). Additionally, I handled the entire puzzle system, including the phrase capturing on the wall, text shaking, and the item pickup and inspection mechanics.
